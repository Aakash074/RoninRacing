About Formula Bharat

Ronin Racing is India's premier Formula Student Electric team with a vision to "Revolutionize Electric Mobility in India focusing on sustainable technologies and innovations" . Formula Bharat is an F1 car design competition in which students from colleges and universities all over India compete with their very own vehicle in areas of engineering design, overall cost, marketability and dynamic performance. Formula Bharat replicates the global Formula Students hosted in around 11 countries per year. The main objective of this competition is to enhance the hands-on practical experience of students while applying engineering concepts taught in the classrooms. Besides this , students can hone up their management skills and build up solidarity which are essential skills required for facing the real-world. These student teams are required to build a vehicle from scratch and seek sponsorship and donations through their own means and contacts to fund their project.

Our Team

To acheive this great project, We need a team from different-different streams

Electrical

power train, low voltage ,differential, acquistion

The members of Electrical Department are in charge of LV safety & harness and data acquisition & dashboard. Electronics & Integration, Battery & BMS and Electrical Power Train come under the umbrella of this department. Besides working on these key components , the department is also involved in vigorous research & development on the emerging developments in electrical racing cars.

Mechanical

Maintaining veicle dynamics ,system dyanamics

The Mechanical Department creates and fabricates chassis, aerodynamics and mechanical parts and develops head vehicle dynamics which includes suspension , steering and braking. This is also responsible for the mechanical power train , one of the critical parts of our Electric Car.

Organizational

Marketing, Sponsorship ,web design

An intense and zestful technical team needs an indistinguishably potent organising team . The organising department manages Media & Public Relations , controls documentation and is responsible for the Team's official website (Web Development). This department brings in sponsors and works as a bridge between alumni,companies and Ronin.
